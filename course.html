Course Summary

This course covers the topics like basic nutrition which every fitness professional should know. This includes information of macro & micro nutrition,the process of digestion & absorption,dietary guidelines & My pyramid.

Next module under this course explains about sports nutrition which will help the trainers to design a customized meal plan according to the exercise schedule.It also covers the supplement recommendations.

The last module specifically focuses on meal planning and precautions to be taken for special population.
