The chicken is a metaphor for the naive heart. So crossing the road, is akin to breaking through an emotional, moral or another self-imposed barrier to reach for fresher horizons. As for why the chicken crossed the road, it could simply be argued that the "chicken", had finally grown the courage to do so.
